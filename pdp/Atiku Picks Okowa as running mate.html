Atiku Picks Okowa as running mate

HE Ifeanyi Okowa, the governor of delta state has been chosen as the running mate to the presidential candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), Atiku Abubakar.

Abubakar posted, “I am delighted to announce Governor Ifeanyi A. Okowa as my Vice Presidential candidate. “I look forward to travelling our great country together, engaging with all Nigerians and building a shared future of peace, unity, and prosperity for all. As One We Can Get It Done. #OneNigeria.” He made the announcement on Thursday via his social media handles.

Okowa was at the PDP Headquarters to attend the party’s Vice Presidential screening. Apart from Okowa, others reportedly shortlisted are Rivers State counterpart, Nyesom Wike and the Akwa Ibom State Governor, Udom Emmanuel.
