Wike tells Anambra Delegate not to waste their vote Peter Obi

Governor of Rivers state, Nyesom Wike has told delegates of the Peoples Democratic Party in Anambra that they should not waste their votes for former governor Peter Obi.

in his statement he said, “I know he will not win. Are you not aware?”, let's tell ourselves the simple truth. i know the one he will win but for this one, no no no mba" He said.
