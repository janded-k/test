Atiku appoints Dino Melaye and Daniel Bwala as campaign spokespersons

Atiku Abubakar the presidential candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party, has appointed Dino Melaye and Daniel Bwala as a spokesperson for his campaign of the 2023 general elections.

Dino Melaye, former senator is from Kogi State, Mr Bwala hails from Adamawa State, same state as the presidential candidate.

The appointments begins immediately according to a statement by Atiku’s spokesperson, Paul Ibe, on Thursday.

The statement reads:

Presidential candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party and former Vice President and former Vice President Atiku Abubakar has appointed Senator Dino Melaye and Dr Daniel Bwala as spokespersons to the Presidential candidate of the PDP for the upcoming presidential campaign.

Meanwhile, Bwala dumped the APC last month over APC candidate, Asiwaju Bola Ahmed Tinubu’s choice of a fellow Muslim, former Borno State Governor, Kashim Shettima as running mate. He however joined the opposition party, the PDP last week.
