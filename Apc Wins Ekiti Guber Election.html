Apc Wins Ekiti Guber Election

The apc candidate Biodun Oyebanji has officially been declared winner of the Ekiti State governorship election

Mr Oyebanji secured 187,057 votes to defeat his closest challengers, Segun Oni of the Social Democratic Party (SDP) who polled 82,211, and Bisi Kolawole of the Peoples Democratic Party who scored 67, 457 votes.
