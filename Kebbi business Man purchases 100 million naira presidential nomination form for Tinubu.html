Kebbi business Man purchases 100 million naira presidential nomination form for Tinubu

The director-general of the Tinubu Support Organisation (TSO), Aminu Suleiman, has signed a N100 million cheque for former Lagos State Governor, Bola Tinubu, to purchase the presidential nomination form of the All Progressives Congress (APC), as this makes Tinubu the first person to purchase the party presidential ticket. The nomination form will sell for N70 million while expression of interest forms will go for N30 million.

The businessman announced via his twitter handle that he has already signed the N100m cheque for Tinubu. He said: “I, Hon. Aminu Suleiman (D.G TSO) will like to officially state that i have signed a Cheque to the tune of 100 million Naira, for the purchase of Nomination and Expression of interest form for our Leader @officialABAT God bless. #BAT2023.”
