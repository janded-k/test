Tinubu Had An Inclosed Door Meeting With Apc Governors

Tinubu had an inclosed door meeting with 12 APC Governors in Abuja. The meeting held was to communicate his vision for the country before formal declaration having met other party stakeholders and leaders of thoughts across the country.

Among those at the meeting are Kaduna Governors Nasir El-Rufai, Governor Mai Mala Buni of Yobe state, Lagos State Governor Babajide Sanwo-Olu. Also attending the meeting are Kwara State Governor Abdulrazaq, who reportedly flew in from the United Kingdom this morning, Plateau State Governor Simon Lalong among others.
