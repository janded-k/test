Apc unveils shettima as running mate

Asiwaju Bola Tinubu of the apc has officially unveiled former Governor of Borno State Kashim Shettima, as his running mate for the 2023 presidential election.

The event was held at the Shehu Musa Yar’Adua Centre in Abuja, on Wednesday alongside other APC chieftains, including the party’s National Chairman, Abdullahi Adamu

This was done after choice of muslim - muslim ticket caused an uproar among the People of the country mostly christians.

During the unveiling ceremony were bishops who Nigerians believed to be fake. The reasons being that christians in the country have rejected Tinubu's choice of Vp as no christians leader would want to be seen at the event.
