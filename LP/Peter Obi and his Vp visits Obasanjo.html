Peter Obi and his Vp visits Obasanjo

the Labour Party presidential candidate, Peter Obi and his running mate, Yusuf Datti Baba-Ahmed, on Sunday visited former president, Olusegun Obasanjo, on Sunday. Also present was the Senior Pastor of Trinity House, Ituah Ighodalo, a social justice activist.

Baba-Ahmed took to his Twitter handle to share pictures from the meeting. The visit may likely be to gather wider acceptance and support for their presidential ambitions.

The tweet read: “Earlier today, myself and H. E. #PeterObi in company of other #NgLabour chieftains consulted with the former President, Olusegun Obasanjo.”
