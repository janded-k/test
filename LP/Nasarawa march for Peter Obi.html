Nasarawa march for Peter Obi

Traders, Youths and supporters of Peter Obi presidential ambition on Saturday stormed commercial activities in Nasarawa as Labour party organised one million man March to register their support for the Labour party and it’s candidate, peter Obi in Lafia.

Speaking, State Chairman of the Party, Alexander Emmanuel said the One-million-man march was to sensitise the people of the state on the need to elect credible leaders in the 2023 as well as sending signal to other political parties who claimed Labour party does not have a structure.

“I accepted the request of the support groups, business class, the youth and members from other parties to embark on this sensitisation rally because I want the people to understand the need why they should elect credible leaders into office in 2023, and it is also to prove a point to other political parties who always say that our supporters are only on the social media.

Meanwhile, other matches will be going in the country for Peter Obi as his supporters look determined to support his presidential ambitiion.
